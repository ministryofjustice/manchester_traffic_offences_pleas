Helping you plead online

A call centre is available for people who cannot use this online service without help.

If you have questions about your case, you'll need to contact your court - not this call centre. You can find the contact details for your court using our court finder service.

Before you call:

  • have the pack we sent with you - you'll be asked to enter information from it
  • be connected to www.makeaplea.service.gov.uk from an internet-enabled device, for example, laptop, tablet or mobile phone

The call centre staff:

  • be able to help you make your plea and/or provide assistance with the court process
  • please note they cannot give you legal advice

Call 0300 303 0656
(Monday - Friday 9am - 5pm)
