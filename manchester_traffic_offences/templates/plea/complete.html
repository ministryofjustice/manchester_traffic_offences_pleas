What now?

You must send us your financial details

By law, you must fill in leaflet MC100 and send it back to us (you’ll find in the pack we sent you). The court will check your financial details when considering your fine.

What happens next?

We’ll send you a letter with the court’s decision on 4th July 2014

You must then:

  • make arrangements to pay your fine within 14 days of us sending the letter
  • send your driving licence and paper counterpart to the DVLA
